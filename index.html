Team Driver Advantage

The majority of our fleet runs two-driver team operations — not solo. This is a critical differentiator for time-sensitive freight.

With team drivers, trucks keep moving around the clock with no mandatory rest stops, meaning a load that would take a solo driver 2-3 days can arrive in 24-36 hours.

For airport linehaul and expedited freight where cut times and on-time delivery are non-negotiable, teams provide the reliability and speed that solo operations simply cannot match.

Fewer delays. Tighter transit windows. Consistent execution.
